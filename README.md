# Premium Car Rental Website

A modern, responsive one-page website for car rental business with multi-language support.

**Live Website:** [https://dhia336.github.io/Car-Renting/](https://dhia336.github.io/Car-Renting/)

## Features

- ✅ **Fully Responsive** - Mobile-first design optimized for all devices
- ✅ **Multi-language Support** - 5 languages (English, Arabic RTL, French, Polish, Czech)
- ✅ **Fast Loading** - Optimized images with lazy loading
- ✅ **Modern UI/UX** - Clean, professional design with smooth animations
- ✅ **Car Showcase** - Beautiful display of 5 premium vehicles
- ✅ **Contact Integration** - WhatsApp and phone call buttons

## Customization

### Update Contact Information

1. **WhatsApp Number**: Update `https://wa.me/1234567890` in:
   - Floating button (line ~154)
   - Contact section (line ~128)

2. **Phone Number**: Update `tel:+1234567890` and displayed number

3. **Email**: Update `info@carrental.com`

4. **Google Maps**: Replace the iframe `src` URL with your business location

### Update Business Information

- Business name: Search and replace "Premium Car Rental" throughout files
- Car prices: Update in `index.html` car cards
- Car specifications: Update in `index.html` car cards
